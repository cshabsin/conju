Chris & Dana's Summer Retreat

at Purity Spring Resort

June 8th-11th, 2023

Eleven years ago we invited some of our friends and family up into the mountains of New Hampshire to hang out for the weekend while we got married. We had so much fun that we're doing it again and skipping the wedding part!

This year our retreat will run from Thursday, June 8th until Sunday, June 11th. Most structured activities will happen between Friday evening and Sunday night. We expect that there will be a small group Thursday night and many more people around Friday - Sunday.

Our goal is to provide a framework for you to have the kind of weekend that would be most fun for you, whether that means hiking and swimming, playing board games and doing jigsaw puzzles, or sitting in a quiet pretty place with a book. If you would like to run an activity, let us know if there is any support you need from us or from Purity Spring.

If you'd like to join us, fill out our RSVP form.

We hope to see you in June! Let us know if you can join us – you can always use the link at the top of this page. Please email us if you have any concerns or questions.
