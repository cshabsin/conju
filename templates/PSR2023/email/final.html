What to Pack

The forecast is sporadically rainy with temperatures in the 60s during the day/high 40s during the night Thursday - Saturday, and sunny and warmer Sunday.

There is an indoor pool and hot tub, and a lake, so there are plenty of opportunities for swimming. PSR provides bath towels and pool towels, but not beach towels.

You'll need to bring whatever you need to feed yourself breakfast (see above).

If you are staying in King Pine you might want to bring a flashlight to make the walk back and forth easier after dark.

The resort has moved to electronic room keys that are also required to get into the common spaces. If you have children who can have some independence but will have trouble handling a key, plan ahead (for example, we're going to give Lydia a lanyard that can hold a key).

If it's easy for you, please bring a checkbook to pay your share of group meals. A lot of money needs to change hands and Venmo/Paypal/Google Pay all have conditions and limits. Sorry!

Also if it's easy, bring a non-breakable, distinct-looking cup, clearly labeled with your name.

We will have plenty of sunscreen and some insect repellant available for communal use. If you are traveling a long distance or don't intend to spend a lot of time outside you should be fine with our supply, but if you have a large family and/or intend to spend the weekend mostly outdoors you may wish to bring your own.

There will probably be lots of ticks. Remember to check yourself and your children when you come in from outside. We'll bring tweezers and rubbing alcohol.

What will help you have fun this weekend? Anything you want to share? Games, jigsaw puzzles, outdoor activities, your knitting, a good book or three? There's a tab for games on the spreadsheet.

Please please please label anything that you intend for communal use with your name so that we can be sure it gets back to you.

As always, you can find more information about our event, or change your profile information or RSVP, on our event website.

If you have any questions or concerns, please let us know. We can't wait to see you all in just a few days!
