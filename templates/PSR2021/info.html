Special Information for 2021

2021-08-16

Less than two weeks to the event! Here's some info you'll need.

To Do Right Now

TL;DR: We need you to do a few things right now :

  • Start thinking about what food you would like for the weekend. The sign up sheet has some details, and there are more details below.
  • If you are joining the Thursday night lobster bake, be sure to make reservations by Tuesday, August 24.
  • Look at the helper spreadsheet and see if you're up for any tasks.
  • Arrange to get a COVID test early enough that you will have results before arriving at PSR.
  • Consider what fun holiday traditions you have missed in the past year (see below).

Food

Food at this year's event will be much more a la carte than it has been in the past. Food service prices at the resort have gone way up, and it seems wise to reduce the risk associated with sharing food between pods.

For each meal we will organize a large group order from a local restaurant. This should make it easy to keep people's food individually packaged. For any meal where you're not interested in the restaurant we chose for the group order, there are plenty of other options within a 20-minute drive, and also some on-site.

Another option is to bring your own snacks and backup food. Every room has a minifridge, microwave, and coffee maker.

There will be two meals with some buffet-style aspects to them:

  • Friday night we're getting pizza. When ordering pizza, you can either order by # of slices (and opt into the communal pool of pizza sharing), or order your own food (pizza or other - the restaurant actually has a lot of options) that will be kept separate.
  • Saturday at lunch we're getting the resort to host the traditional cookout.

One of the options for Thursday dinner is a Lobster Bake that Purity Spring runs. They have other options besides lobster. The Lobster Bake requires advance reservations. To reserve a spot at the Lobster Bake, you need to make a reservation no later than Tuesday, 8/24 -- call 603-367-8896 and dial 0 for the front desk.

To help reduce confusion during the weekend, consider bringing a glass or mug that is distinctive-looking and clearly labeled with your name. If you're feeling extra waste-conscious, you could bring a plate and bowl too. We will bring disposable dishes and silverware, but it would be nice not to go through 600 red solo cups in the space of 4 days.

Help!

Between the food situation and the pandemic situation, the logistical burden of running the weekend is much higher than normal. If you can spare some time and energy during the weekend, or if you have equipment you can bring for us to share, we'd really appreciate it.

Please look at the helper spreadsheet and see if you're up for any tasks.

Pandemic

It's hard to have a fun weekend if people don't feel safe. As mentioned the other day, we are taking several precautions:

  • We'd like everyone to get a negative COVID test result before coming up.
  • We'll have tests on hand for anyone who needs one (to a point).
  • We'd like everyone to keep masks on when in the common room (the "Fireplace Room").
  • We'll try to eat meals outside as much as possible (weather permitting). There are several picnic tables just outside our building.
  • We're working on getting some HEPA air purifiers to set up in the common room.

We've gotten some questions about whether there will be space for people who are comfortable being unmasked; we are attempting to convince PSR to give us some space suitable for this purpose.

If you have COVID-related concerns that aren't addressed here, please let us know.

Rooming

We have had a few cancellations since the initial rooming assignments went out, so we have had to move some people around. If this applies to you, we've probably already talked to you about it, unless the swap was mostly unremarkable. We might need your help to move room reservations around to make the resort's records match reality.

Rides

We haven't forgotten about this; keep an eye out for email about this if you told us you need a ride, or if you offered a ride.
